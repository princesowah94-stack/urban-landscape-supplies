    Delivery FAQs

    We deliver across the Sydney metro area from our Wetherill Park depot. Zone A covers local Western Sydney including Fairfield, Liverpool, Smithfield, Merrylands and surrounds. Zone B covers Parramatta, Blacktown, Bankstown, Campbelltown, Penrith and Sutherland Shire. Zone C covers the Inner West, Eastern Suburbs, North Shore and Northern Beaches. Contact us if your suburb isn't listed — we may still be able to deliver.
    Free delivery thresholds by zone: Local Western Sydney (Zone A) — free on orders over $150. Greater Western Sydney (Zone B) — free on orders over $200. Eastern & Northern Sydney (Zone C) — free on orders over $300. These thresholds apply to standard bagged product orders. Bulk yard orders use a separate quote-based delivery cost.
    Local Western Sydney (Zone A): 1–2 business days. Greater Western Sydney (Zone B): 2–3 business days. Eastern & Northern Sydney (Zone C): 3–5 business days. Delivery timeframes are estimates and may vary during peak periods. You'll receive a confirmation email with your estimated delivery window once your order is placed.
    Standard deliveries run Monday to Friday. Saturday delivery is available for select Metro Core suburbs at an additional surcharge of $20. Contact us when placing your order to request a Saturday delivery slot — subject to availability.
    Yes. Bulk yard materials (soil, mulch, sand, gravel by the cubic metre or tonne) are delivered by tipper truck. Delivery pricing for bulk materials is calculated based on your suburb and order size — use our bulk quote form to get a price. Minimum orders apply.
    Call us on 1300 872 267 as soon as possible after placing your order. We can update your delivery address if the order hasn't yet been dispatched. Once an order is with our driver, address changes may not be possible.
