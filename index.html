Common Questions

Landscaping Supplies Sydney — FAQs

What landscaping supplies do you deliver in Sydney?

We deliver mulch, garden soil, decorative pebbles, sand and bulk yard materials as 1-tonne bulk bags to 66 suburbs across the Sydney metro area. The range includes hardwood chip mulch, premium garden mix, snow-white pebbles, charcoal grey pebbles, river sand and premium turf underlay soil.

How quickly can you deliver landscape supplies in Sydney?

We deliver to 66 Sydney suburbs. Same-day collection is also available from our Wetherill Park depot at 21/130 Hassall Street.

Which Sydney suburbs do you deliver to?

We deliver across 66 suburbs including Parramatta, Blacktown, Penrith, Liverpool, Fairfield, Campbelltown, Bankstown, Auburn, Strathfield, Merrylands, Granville, the Inner West, Eastern Suburbs and the North Shore. See the full delivery area list or enter your postcode in the checker above to confirm availability.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery is free on orders over $150 within our Sydney metro delivery zone. A standard delivery fee applies to orders under $150. All products ship as 1-tonne bulk bags. For large or multi-bag commercial orders, request a bulk quote for discounted pricing.

Do you offer trade accounts for landscapers and contractors?

Yes. We offer trade accounts for landscapers, builders and contractors with discounted pricing, priority delivery and full catalogue access. We currently support over 500 active trade accounts. Learn more about trade accounts.
