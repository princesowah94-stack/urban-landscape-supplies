Our Story

Western Sydney's Landscape Supply Specialists

Urban Landscape Supplies has been the trusted source for premium landscaping materials across Sydney since 2010. Based in Wetherill Park in Western Sydney, what began as a single yard operation has grown into one of Sydney's most respected landscape supply businesses — serving homeowners, landscapers, builders and councils alike.

We're proudly Australian family owned and operated, and that means something to us. Every product we stock, every delivery we make, and every customer we serve reflects our commitment to quality, reliability and genuine care.
